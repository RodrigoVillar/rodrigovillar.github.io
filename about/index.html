About.

My name is Rodrigo and I am currently a 3rd year student at Cornell University.

Outside my academic studies in computer science and mathematics, I am deeply intrested in the field that is blockchain. I believe that Satoshi Nakamoto's publication of the Bitcoin whitepaper in 2008 was the beginning of a technological revolution that is only rivaled by two other events in recent history: the creation of the world wide web and the introduction of the personal computer.

As of currently, I am the co-Head of Research at Cornell Blockchain, where I am responsible for leading technical research which the goal of positively contributing to the field of blockchain. In the past, I was a intern for CrossTower, where I was tasked with automating the firm's daily risk report and producing visualizations of perpetual correlations for the trading desk.

Outside of academia/blockchain, I enjoy working out, playing football (soccer) with my hometown friends, and taking my dog Lucho on walks.
